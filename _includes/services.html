To move things faster, some personnalities are starting to raise their voices to make the world change. However, even though her image and her thoughts were widely spread in the media, did the people really listened to her reclamation ? Did they become more interested by the topic? Did she made, indeed, people more aware that they needed to find out more about climate change?
